Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember certain information about your visit — such as items in your cart — to improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

Worldwide Wholesalers uses only functional cookies that are essential for the site to work correctly. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics cookies that monitor your behaviour across other websites.

Cookies We Use

Name Type Purpose Duration
ww_cart Functional Stores your shopping cart items so they persist between page visits and browser sessions. Persistent (localStorage — no expiry, cleared when you place an order or clear browser data)

Note: ww_cart is stored in localStorage, not a traditional cookie file, but serves the same functional purpose.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking scripts. If you click through to our social media pages (Instagram, Facebook) or WhatsApp, those platforms may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Removing the ww_cart localStorage entry will clear your shopping cart. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies and localStorage data are stored
  • Delete individual entries or all stored data
  • Block cookies from specific sites

Please note that disabling functional storage may affect how the shopping cart and other site features work.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add new features or integrations. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Further Information

For more details on how we handle your personal data, see our Privacy Policy. If you have questions, please contact us via the details on our home page.
