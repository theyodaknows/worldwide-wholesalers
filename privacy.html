Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Worldwide Wholesalers ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you use our website at worldwidewholesalers.store.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you submit a quote request:

  • Business name and contact name
  • Email address
  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Optional notes about your order (colour preferences, sizes, delivery country)

We also collect limited technical data automatically when you visit our site, including IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited. This is standard server log data retained for security and performance purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your wholesale quote requests via WhatsApp or email
  • To communicate with you about your orders and enquiries
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Quote request data is transmitted directly to our team via WhatsApp and is not stored on our web servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information during transmission.

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry or as required by applicable law.

Third-Party Services

Our site uses the following third-party services that may process limited data:

  • WhatsApp (Meta) — for quote delivery and communication. Subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash — for product imagery. Images served directly from their CDN.
  • A2 Hosting — our web host. Subject to their data processing terms.

Cookies

We use only functional cookies necessary for the site to operate, including storing your shopping cart. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email (see contact details on our home page).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out via the contact details on our home page.
